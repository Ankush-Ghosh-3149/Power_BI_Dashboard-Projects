## Introduction

This project was designed to bridge the gap between **forecasting accuracy and operational execution by combining demand analytics, inventory performance, risk assessment, and actionable decision intelligence** within a single interactive Power BI solution.

## Skills Showcased

- ⚙️ **Data Transformation (ETL) with Power Query:** Cleaned, shaped, and prepared the raw data for analysis by handling blanks, changing data types, and creating new columns.

- 📱 **Implicit Measures:** Formulated measures to derive key insights and KPIs like Median Yearly Salary and Job Count`.

- 📊 **Core Charts:** Utilized **Column, Bar, Line,**
and **Box Charts** to compare job counts and track trends over time.

- 📈 **KPI Indicators & Tables:** Used **Cards** to display key metrics and **Tables** to provide granular, sortable data.

- 🎨 **Dashboard Design:** Designed an intuitive and visually appealing layout, exploring both common and uncommon chart types to best tell the data story.

- 🖱️ **Interactive Reporting:**
  - **Slicers:** To dynamically filter the report by Job Title.

  - **Buttons & Bookmarks:** To create a seamless navigation experience.

  - **Drill-Through:** To navigate from a high-level summary to a contextual, detailed view.

## Dashboard Overview

### Page 1: HOME

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%201.png)

This is the gateway to the Demand Forecasting & Inventory Analytics Dashboard. It introduces the business problem, outlines the dashboard structure, and provides intuitive navigation to help users explore demand trends, forecasting performance, operational impact, and decision insights.

### Page 2: Executive Overview

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%202.png)

This is the command center of the Demand Forecasting & Inventory Analytics Dashboard. It provides a high-level view of demand trends, inventory allocation, service performance, forecast accuracy, and operational efficiency across regions, enabling stakeholders to quickly assess business health and identify areas requiring attention.

### Page 3: Forecast Performance

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%203.png)

This page evaluates the accuracy and reliability of forecasting models by analyzing forecast errors, variability, confidence levels, and category-wise performance. It helps identify where predictions are deviating from actual demand and highlights areas requiring forecasting improvements.

### Page 4: Operations Impact

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%204.png)

This page examines how forecasting performance translates into real operational outcomes by analyzing inventory levels, holding costs, stockouts, lost sales, and regional inefficiencies. It helps stakeholders understand the business consequences of forecast inaccuracies and inventory decisions.

### Page 5: Decision Intelligence

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%205.png)

This page transforms analytical insights into actionable business decisions by combining forecast performance, inventory efficiency, operational risk, and financial impact. Through a decision matrix and recommendation framework, it helps stakeholders prioritize high-impact actions, mitigate supply chain risks, and optimize resource allocation. It serves as the final decision-support layer of the dashboard.

### Page 6: Performance & Health Overview

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%206.png)

This page provides a consolidated assessment of overall supply chain health by tracking key performance indicators, forecast reliability, inventory effectiveness, and operational efficiency. It enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify emerging risks, and evaluate whether strategic objectives are being achieved. By combining multiple dimensions into a single view, it offers a quick and comprehensive measure of organizational performance.

### Page 7: Deep Analysis

![Dashboard Home Page](/Images/P1/P1%20Image%207.png)

This page enables detailed investigation of specific regions, product categories, and time periods through interactive drill-through analysis. It provides a contextual view of demand trends, forecast performance, error distributions, and forecast gaps to uncover the root causes behind operational outcomes. By moving from summary insights to granular data, stakeholders can perform targeted analysis and make more informed decisions.
